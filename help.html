Using the Search Feature

The search bar helps you locate information quickly. To search:

  1. Tap or click inside the search box.
  2. Type a keyword or phrase.
  3. Matching results will appear automatically.
  4. Select any result to open the corresponding section immediately.

Examples of searchable keywords include: Education, Primary School, High School, Leadership, Football, Manchester United, Barcelona, Health, Faith, Gallery, Contact,
The search is not case-sensitive, meaning uppercase and lowercase letters produce the same results.
